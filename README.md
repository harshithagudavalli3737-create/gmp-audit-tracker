**Tools:** Python · pandas · NumPy · Matplotlib · openpyxl  

---

## Overview

A risk-based internal audit tracking system that maps 55 GMP requirements 
to specific 21 CFR Part 211 sections across 6 departments — evaluating 
compliance, classifying findings by risk level, calculating weighted 
compliance scores, and generating a professional 4-sheet Excel audit report.

---

## What This Project Covers

- 55 audit requirements mapped to specific 21 CFR Part 211 sections
- 6 departments audited: QA, Production, QC, Warehouse, Maintenance, Document Control
- Weighted compliance scoring by requirement criticality (weight 1–3)
- Finding classification: Critical, Major, or Observation
- Department-level compliance scores with risk ratings
- 4-sheet Excel audit report with embedded compliance chart
- Executive summary with overall compliance score
